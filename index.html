Hong Kong is a special administrative region of China, famous for its skyscrapers with fabulous views. It has many interesting places to try out and do in it.




When to go and how to get there

The best time to go to this wonderful place is during March and April or October and November as during summer is very hot and humid but between winter and summer it is the perfect temperature and conditions to go to.

The best way and fastest to reach Hong Kong is via airplanes, with just the right booking and cost you can get yourself a comfortable travel experience.

Things to see and do

The best places to see are the following:

  • Victoria Peak
  • Disneyland
  • Tian Tan Buddha
  • Star Ferry
  • Ocean Park
  • Ngong Ping
  • Nan Lin Garden
  • Hong Kong Skyline
